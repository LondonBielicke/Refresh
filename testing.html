About Us

We are high school students in the greater St. Louis area with growing passion and ability to code. Sparking our coding superpowers was a camp called Kode with Klossy, a non-profit started by supermodel Karlie Kloss. During our second year in level 2 we were placed in our final projects as teamates and worked to create this website, which we later built out and submitted to built by girls. Now we are here, pitching our idea to influencers and building our brand, Refresh.
